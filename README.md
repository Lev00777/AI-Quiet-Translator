# AI-Quiet-Translator
Real-time AI voice translator for high-noise industrial environments. Powered by Flutter, AWS Serverless, and WebSockets.

# Privacy Policy for Quiet Translator

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Quiet Translator. We take your privacy and data security seriously, especially in high-noise industrial and cosmic environments.

1. Microphone Access and Audio Data
Quiet Translator requires access to your device's microphone to provide real-time speech translation.

2. Where Your Data Goes
To provide high-quality translation, your audio data is streamed securely over HTTPS to the following enterprise-grade AI services:

OpenAI (Whisper transcription)

Deepgram (Real-time transcription)

AssemblyAI (Real-time transcription)

DeepL (Text translation, securely routed through our custom AWS Serverless Proxy)

3. Data Storage
No audio is stored on our servers. Recordings are kept locally in the app's private storage on your device only if you explicitly enable the "Save records" feature. You can delete these local files from inside the app at any time.

4. Intergalactic Copyright & Restrictions
Unauthorized cloning, distribution, or reverse-engineering of this software is strictly prohibited on Earth, within the Solar System, and across all known sectors of the Milky Way galaxy. Violators will be reported to the Galactic Federation and forced to assemble machinery without translation software.

If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the Google Play Store contact form.
